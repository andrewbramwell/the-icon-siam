Our Story

About The Icon Siam

Welcome to The Icon Siam, where Khun Nang brings authentic Thai healing to Potters Bar. Originally from Nakhon Ratchasima, she established our centre in May 2025 after experiencing firsthand how proper massage techniques can effectively treat pain.

Our small, friendly team holds qualifications in Swedish Massage, Sports Massage, Aromatherapy and Traditional Thai Massage. We focus on addressing specific conditions rather than offering one-size-fits-all treatments.

We believe in doing things properly. Each visit begins with a comprehensive intake form, ensuring your treatment targets your exact needs. Our clean, comfortable space and attentive care create the ideal environment for your healing journey.

We believe effective therapy begins with listening to your body and responding with skilled touch. Nang Owner
