About Siam Thai Spa

Established in 2019, Siam Thai Spa brings the authentic healing traditions of Thailand to Cricklewood, North West London. Founded by Nadia, a skilled therapist from Udon Thani with over a decade of experience, our spa specialises in genuine Thai massage techniques that effectively address chronic pain and mobility issues. Our commitment to authenticity and therapeutic results has made us a trusted destination for those seeking relief from physical discomfort.
